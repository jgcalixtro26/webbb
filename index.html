Student | Leader | Advocate

I am an undergraduate student taking up B Library and Information Science (BLIS), a DOST-recognized degree program under the Math, Science, and Technology (MST) domain, focusing on the integration of web development and management of various information repositories. I am studying at the University of the Philippines Diliman - School of Library and Information Studies , the first and only CHED Center of Excellence in LIS Education and the oldest library school in the country.

I have 5 months experience working in an American payroll and currently serving as a part-time learning facilitator of the Pilipinas Shell Foundation Inc.,teaching science high school students all over the Philippines the learning and discovery tools developed by Shell International.
